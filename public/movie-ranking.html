The Best Movies According To me

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Darling

Prabha prepares to meet Nandini,his childhood friend, whom he expects to see soon at the reunion party thrown by his father.But,turning down a gangster's daughter's overtures gets him in trouble.

One Peice

Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew explore a fantastical world of endless oceans and exotic islands in search of the world's ultimate treasure to become the next Pirate King.

KGF

Rocky, a young man, seeks power and wealth in order to fulfil a promise to his dying mother. His quest takes him to Mumbai, where he gets involved with the notorious gold mafia.
